Career

I started out of college doing control system programming for a large Audio Visual company. They where looking to integrate modern programming practices into an progromatically lagging industry. After getting my Crestron Master certification and lacking any sort of career trajectory I moved to the largest AV company where I saw an actual career path. Not long after I got my Crestron Master - Silver level certification. I got an offer to move to another company to lead their programming department, my dream job, and took it. Shortly after Covid-19 hit and the company struggled trying to make sales and eventually started letting 90% of the company go over the next year. I was caught at the tail end of the layoffs leaving just the sales staff and some design engineers left. After landing a new job the effects of covid was not just effecting new project opportunities but also causing supply chain problems. This left me without a job for the second time and caused me to start pursing a career outside the AV field. I attended a coding bootcamp to brush up on programming changes of the past 8 years and pick up some new skills to help me enter a new part of my career.
